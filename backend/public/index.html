What Epheme Is

Epheme is a vision for software that respects people more than metrics. A place for developers who feel the weight of modern SaaS and want something quieter, simpler, and more sovereign.

We are growing this into a collection of open resources, reference projects, and practical patterns for building privacy-focused software with less platform baggage.

Why This Exists

Most software today assumes it needs to know you: your name, your history, your behavior. Epheme is a reaction to that.

  • local-first
  • privacy-respecting
  • ephemeral
  • playful
  • unburdened by corporate gravity

Not everything needs to scale. Not everything needs to remember.

What You Will Find Here

We are building a collection of resources for developers who want to build software that is privacy-first, local-first, and free of unnecessary platform weight.

This is a work in progress. The ideas are here. The tools and guides are being shaped over time — in the open, without a roadmap or a deadline.

Check back as things take shape, or join the conversation on Reddit.

How We Build

  1. Local First

    Your device is enough. No cloud unless absolutely necessary.

  2. No Accounts

    If a tool needs your identity, it probably is not an Epheme tool.

  3. No Tracking

    We do not watch, measure, or profile. We build tools, not funnels.

  4. Ephemeral by Default

    When you are done, the tool should let go. No history. No baggage.

  5. Playful, Not Noisy

    Warm, human, developer-friendly. Never corporate.

What Epheme Is Not

  • a startup
  • a growth engine
  • a platform chasing lock-in
  • a brand hunting attention
  • a place for heavy identity systems

If it feels clingy, extractive, or overbuilt, it is not Epheme.

The Invitation

Epheme is open to anyone building software that is privacy-first, local-first, and human-scale. Bring what you are making. Learn from shared resources. Help shape what better software can feel like.

Build freely. Leave no trace.
